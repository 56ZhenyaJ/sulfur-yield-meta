# Screening rules

## Include

- Experimental studies on crop yield response to sulfur-containing fertilizer.
- Field, pot, greenhouse, or controlled agronomic trials.
- Studies reporting mean yield for sulfur treatment and a comparator.
- Studies where yield can be converted to t/ha or consistently analyzed in original units.
- Sulfur alone or sulfur co-applied with other nutrients.

## Exclude

- Reviews without extractable experimental data.
- Soil incubation studies without crop yield.
- Studies where sulfur is inseparable from a non-comparable treatment package and no sensitivity coding is possible.
- Studies only reporting nutrient concentration without yield or biomass outcome.
- Non-agricultural sulfur applications.

## Priority coding

Use this order when selecting contrasts:

1. `s_vs_no_s_same_background`: sulfur is the only treatment difference.
2. `s_only_vs_absolute_control`: sulfur alone versus no sulfur or unfertilized control.
3. `package_vs_absolute_control`: full fertilizer package versus control; keep only for sensitivity analysis.
